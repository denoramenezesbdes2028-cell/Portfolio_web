Hi, I’m Denora Menezes , a communication design student who views design as a language beyond words. To me, design is the purest way to express what we feel and to tell stories that truly connect with people. It’s not just about visuals , it’s about emotion, intent, and impact. I believe design has the power to simplify the complex, to make everyday life easier, and to solve problems creatively while staying deeply human. Every project I work on blends storytelling, empathy, and purpose, because I see good design not just as something that looks beautiful, but as something that makes you feel, think, and understand better.
